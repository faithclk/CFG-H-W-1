Pitta Pizza

It may be questionable as to whether these can actually be classed as pizza, but these are super easy and tasty to whip up! 😋

Here is the recipe:

Obtain ingredients that you like on a pizza, tomato sauce and the key ingredient, pitta breads 🫓

I also like to make a homemade tomato sauce, just a tinned tomato can, red onion, garlic sauce with seasoning that I leave to simmer for a while

Chop up cheese and prep other ingredients for the pizza

My favourites are olives, pepperoni, mushrooms, mozzarella cheese 😋

Then cook in the oven on a medium high heat setting for 10 minutes or until the pitta breads are crisp and the cheese is golden
